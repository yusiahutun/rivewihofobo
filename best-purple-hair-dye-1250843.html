    Now that you have checked the products, here's how to pick the best one among them -

    How to Pick the Best Purple Hair Dye?

    Below are the parameters to keep in mind before buying the best purple hair dye - 

    a) Ingredients to avoid- Ingredients like parabens, phthalates, ammonia, and sulfates must be avoided to ensure good health of your hair. Natural dyes with conditioning agents are the best to dye your hair with.

    b) Hair Color- Your own hair color plays the most important role in the outcome of the dye. If you already have dark hair, it is almost impossible to get the desired vibrancy with an average product without bleaching your hair first. In this scenario, picking a product that is equipped to work on dark hair is necessary to obtain the desired result.

    e) Staying Power- There are multiple options when it comes to hair dyes - permanent, semi-permanent, and touch-ups. While permanent hair dyes last for 6-8 weeks, semi-permanent hair colors will last only a few washes. On the other hand, touch-ups will increase the longevity of your hair dyes.

    d) Shades- There are several shades of purple available for you to try out. In short, the color palette offers something for everyone! If you want a soothing shade, lavender and lilac are the colors for you. For the ones who want to make a quirky statement, violet and neon colors could be the best option. If you want to avail the safest option, eggplant color is the one!

    e) Usability- If you want a fuss-free approach, plain creme or spray is the safest option for you that doesn't include aggressive mixing. But if you want a professional-like result, an all-in-one kit is what you need.

    f) Fading- Dark colors like purple fade away and turn out to be a different color like blonde, green, blue, pink, etc. Before you choose your shade, make sure you are aware of the secondary shade you're settling in for.

    Now, take a look at how you can apply a purple hair dye effectively—

    How to Apply Purple Hair Dye?

    Here's how you can apply a purple hair dye effectively at home - 

    a) If you have a darker hair color, bleaching your hair is ideal to get the desired vibrancy.

    b) Apply coconut hair oil a night before applying bleach to your hair. 

    c) After you are done with the bleaching process, leave it for the recommended amount of time and later rinse your hair with water.

    d) Now, wear gloves to avoid staining.

    e) Mix the ingredients present in the package of the dye as per the directions.

    f) Apply it over your hair and keep it for 45 minutes or the recommended amount of time in the package. 

    g) Later, rinse the color and apply shampoo followed by a conditioner to unveil the bright color.

    Below are a few tips to maintain a purple hair color—

    How to Maintain Purple Dyed Hair?

    a) First thing first, after dying your hair, you must choose shampoos and conditioners that protect the hair color and don't let them fade.

    b) Minimizing the usage of hot tools will also keep the dyed hair in check and resist fading.

    c) Using a heat or UV protectant serum will go a long way and ensure the longevity of your dyed hair.

    d) Since the hair gets dry right after dying, it is recommended to use a nourishing mask to restore the moisture into your hair.

    e) Oiling your hair once a week will also work wonders to keep the dry and damaged tresses at bay.

    f) Touch-up is one of the greatest ways to keep the hair vibrant for the longest time.

    Now, the million-dollar question is how you can remove the purple hair dye. Fret not, we have got you covered! Below are the points to keep in mind -

    How to Remove Purple Hair Dye?

    Here's how you can do it if you don't like the outcome at all—

    a) Color Remover- Chemical color remover is the most potent option when it comes to removing the color from your hair. There are plenty of products available in the market that you can try at your home to safely remove the color all by yourself. 

    b) Bleach Wash- Opting for bleach wash will tone down your hair and wash away the purple pigment. However, it is to be kept in mind that bleach wash permanently damages and changes your natural hair color, unlike semi-permanent hair colors.

    c) Gold-tone Dye- Using a gold or yellow tone dye will neutralize the effect of purple hair. If it is not much of a big deal to use a gold or yellow tone to your hair, you can definitely neutralize your hair using one.

    FAQ's

    Can I apply purple hair dye over dark brown hair?

    Yes, you can! However, the outcome will not be as vibrant as gray or blonde hair will have. But if you want that vibrancy, opting for bleach will be wise for you.

    Which purple hair dye doesn't fade fast?

    Garnier Nutrisse Ultra Color, L'oreal Paris Feria High-intensity Shimmering Color, or Lime Crime Unicorn Hair Dye are ideal choices for fade-resistance hair dyes.

    Why does purple hair fade so fast?

    This is because the molecules of the purple hair dye are not equipped enough to reach deep into the hair shaft.

    Is there a permanent purple hair dye?

    The permanent hair dyes typically start to fade after 6-8 weeks or until the roots start to grow. So, the answer is no!

    How often can I dye my hair purple?

    You should ideally have a gap of 6-8 weeks before dying your hair again.

    Is it possible to dye my hair purple without bleaching it first?

    Yes, there are brands that offer intense vibrancy and ensure a good color payoff without bleaching.

    Can I mix different shades of purple hair dye to create a custom color?

    Yes, you can mix semi-permanent hair dyes in a bowl to build a custom color. Or, you can apply multiple shades on your hair shaft to create an ombre effect.

    How can I prevent my purple hair color from fading quickly?

    Following a few precautions like using a heat protectant, minimizing the use of hot tools, and not exposing too much sunshine, may help prevent your purple hair color from fading quickly.

    Can I apply purple hair dye on top of previously dyed hair?

    Yes, you can definitely dye overdye! However, make sure the gap is 2-3 months at least to minimize the damage.

    Are there any specific precautions I should take when using purple hair dye during pregnancy or if I have allergies?

    If you have allergies, you should always do a patch test before applying the color to your hair. On the other hand, if you are pregnant, you should consult your doctor before dying your hair or at least wait until the second trimester before doing the same.
