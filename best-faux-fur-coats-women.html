Raey Exaggerated Shoulder Bomber Jacket

1

Best Faux-Fur Bomber

Raey Exaggerated Shoulder Bomber Jacket

The tones on this Raey bomber jacket may be neutral, but the bold coat is anything but subtle. For the budding street-style star, this coat stands out from the crowd. It’s made with a blend of alpaca, wool, and camel, meaning it will keep you cozy even in cold temperatures.

Currently, she is most drawn to sleek silhouettes: “Anything classic that feels very Carrie Bradshaw à la [Sex and the City] season one.”

Materials: 67% polyester, 33% plant-based fiber
