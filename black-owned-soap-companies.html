The Black-Owned Soap Companies We Love to Shop 2022

1

Alaffia EveryDay Shea Hand Soap - Peppermint Tingle

1

Alaffia EveryDay Shea Hand Soap - Peppermint Tingle

You know a hand soap's going to be good when it's called Peppermint Tingle. An experience as well as a cleanser, this minty formula is made completely without sulfates, making it less likely to dry out your precious hands. 

2

Bevel Natural Bar Soap

2

Bevel Natural Bar Soap

Almost too stylish to use, Bevel's natural body bar includes exfoliants like pumice and jojoba seed powder, so your skin glows brighter and ingredients like cocoa and shea butter can really get in there and moisturize. 

3

Sade Baron Vulcano | Activated Charcoal Bar Soap

3

Sade Baron Vulcano | Activated Charcoal Bar Soap

Activated charcoal soaps are a staple in the clean beauty community, but this bar makes the ingredient even better by adding in coconut oil, shea butter, tea tree oil, and peppermint oil. Body acne doesn't stand a chance.

4

Organic Bath Co Zesty Morning Organic Body Wash

4

Organic Bath Co Zesty Morning Organic Body Wash

Wake up and smell the oranges. Indulge in this delightfully zesty—and sulfate-free—body wash on the mornings where coffee isn't doing the trick. 

5

Hunny Bunny Goats Milk, Oats and Hunny

5

Hunny Bunny Goats Milk, Oats and Hunny

Small and family-focused, the boutique soap and personal care brand Hunny Bunny is as cute as it is powerful. We have our eye on this adorable goat milk bar, which includes a buffet of vitamins and benefits for your skin.  

6

JACQ's Yucca & Lavender Cleansing Bar

6

JACQ's Yucca & Lavender Cleansing Bar

Bar soaps and cleansing bars aren't typically known for being super friendly for sensitive skin. JACQ's is changing that though, thanks to this gentle bar, which can be used all over your body.

7

Blade + Bloom Rich Cleansing Bar/Brighter Than Sunshine

7

Blade + Bloom Rich Cleansing Bar/Brighter Than Sunshine

Look no further if you buy indie soap for the scents. Blade + Bloom fragrances are so unique—like this lemongrass-infused Brighter Than Sunshine bar, which smells like a "sunset on a beach in Thailand."  

8

Shea Radiance African Black Soap Body Wash

8

Shea Radiance African Black Soap Body Wash

Alternatively, you might prefer unscented products, and Shea Radiance has your back if that's the case. It offers its body wash in Lavender & Rosemary and Citrus & Spearmint combinations as well as in unscented. 

9

PUR Home 34 oz Dish Soap

9

PUR Home 34 oz Dish Soap

As one of the soaps your hands interact with the most, a skin-friendly dish soap can be the one thing that stands between silky, baby-soft hands and cracked, dried-out skin. This pick from PUR Home is made from biodegradable ingredients and essential oils you can feel better about.
