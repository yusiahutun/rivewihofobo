The Most Romantic Month of the Year

After April’s chaotic combo of eclipse season and Mercury retrograde, your May monthly horoscope for each zodiac sign will be a breath of fresh air. For the first few weeks of the month, Taurus season will help you slow down and get grounded, but there are still plenty of surprises in store with disruptive and revolutionary Uranus leading the way.

Pluto retrograde begins on May 2, heralding in a period of subtle subterranean processing, as well as marking the end of this year’s final retrograde-free streak. On May 7, a gorgeous new moon in Taurus will remind you that abundance is there for the taking, so long as you’re willing to step up and tap in. Plant some seeds and take advantage of this fertile cosmic energy.

Taurus energy really reigns supreme once logistics planet Mercury ingresses into this fixed earth sign on May 15. Embrace methodical ways of thinking and focus on making slow but steady progress toward your goals. The Taurus energy really comes to a climax on May 18, when the sun aligns with lucky planet Jupiter to form a Jupiter cazimi. This is a magical cosmic moment to think big, start something new, or take a leap of faith toward something that’s been calling your name. And as romantic Venus joins forces with insightful and unpredictable Uranus on the same day, you’ll be given an irresistible opportunity to fall in love with something—or someone—at first sight.

Speaking of new beginnings, Gemini season starts on May 20, setting our minds aflutter with curiosities and a sense of mental connection. It’s a wonderful time to catch up with friends and make the most of spring. On May 23, the full moon in Sagittarius pushes us to expand our horizons and set our sights on bigger and brighter things while a Venus-Jupiter conjunction causes sparks to fly in love and money.

However, the most momentous cosmic event of the month comes on May 25, when planetary big-shot Jupiter leaves behind Taurus and enters Gemini. It’s been more than 10 years since auspicious Jupiter last made its way through Gemini, and this ingress marks the start of a new cycle of opportunity and abundance for all zodiac signs. We’re collectively expanding our means of connecting with the world around us, developing exciting new ideas, and embracing a greater sense of open-mindedness. As we ride into the last astrological stretch of spring, we’re truly ushering in a brand-new era.
