Rosie Huntington-Whiteley's Jimmy Choo boots

Back in September, Rosie Huntington-Whiteley shared one of our favourite outfits yet. Posing in her signature stance, the model showed off a chic knitted top (from Mango) and khaki skirt combo, but it was her Jimmy Choo boots that stole the show.

The knee-high design is from the brand's autumn/winter 2019 collection, which fashion editors everywhere are currently coveting. You've no doubt seen the new Jimmy Choo monogram logo, debuted this season, everywhere on Instagram.

Why are these boots quite so desirable, you might ask? Well, they hit the sweet spot between buzzy and classic; tapping into this season's fascination with the '70s bourgeois with their tan hue and square toe, as well as fashion's perennial penchant for all things luxury logo-adorned.

Instagram

No doubt the price tag proves something of an obstacle when it comes to snap-decision purchases, so we're pleased to see the boots are in Net-a-porter's Boxing Day sale.

The luxury e-tailer currently has up to 60% off designer favourites in its sale edit – which is packed with new-season pieces from the likes of Victoria Beckham, Marni, Max Mara, The Frankie Shop, Erdem and The Vampire's Wife.
